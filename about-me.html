I was formerly a product brochure copywriter for a company called InventHelp. When the rampid spread of COVID-19 forced many Americans to social distance, I knew it was an ideal time to foster new skills.

Everyone knows Shakespeare wrote King Lear while forced to quarantine during the bubonic plague. What could I do?

I began reading about the fundamentals of programming and even tried my hand at a few basic javascript apps. I made fun and simple games, madlibs and tic-tac-toe. I want to do more, and I want to work with great programmers.

I believe that the user experience should be about about more than getting people to camp in rabbit holes for long hours. As my skills develop I want to make the web more accessible for all individuals. The world wide web opened doors for so many people. I want to open doors for so many more.
