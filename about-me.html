I was formerly a product brochure copywriter for a company called InventHelp. When the rampant spread of COVID-19 forced many Americans to social distance, I knew it was an ideal time to foster new skills.

Everyone knows Shakespeare wrote King Lear while forced to quarantine during the bubonic plague. What could I do?

I began reading about the fundamentals of programming and even tried my hand at a few basic javascript apps. I made fun and simple games, madlibs and tic-tac-toe. I want to do more, and I want to work with great programmers.

My knowledge base is growing quickly. Let me work with you and your team to create an ideal user experience. I am skilled in the essential mark up languages HTML and CSS. I have substantial practice using JS libraries and frameworks such as jQuery and React. Because my program is remote I am incredibly comfortable navigating the command line to deploy apps and sites using Git.

As my skills develop I want to make the web more accessible for all individuals. The worldwide web opened doors for so many people. I want to open doors for so many more.
